Influencer Sai Krishan Kumar frequently interacts with Intel and has become quite the brand ambassador.

DPG CHANNELS COMMUNITY MANAGEMENT

(Intel Business, Intel HPC, Intel 5G Networks)

 

 

 

  

 

As we work internally on some potential upcoming Intel led Twitter Spaces, we wanted to check in with our audience and see if this is something they would be interested in.

 

We posted a poll & saw great feedback with over 60 votes and a few comments on what topics we should address. We also noticed that a lot of people did not know what Spaces were, so we followed up with the FAQ link for them to learn more. On top of that Twitter Spaces updated this week a new feature to schedule Spaces in advance. We commented on this update as one of the only brands and also followed with a few key Twitter employees who created Spaces who then followed us back.

 

Once we are set to test out Spaces, actions like this help put us in good graces with Twitter to help amplify and manage them.

 

 

 

Top Community Engagement

 

  • Sarah Good and Stephanie Hallford were featured in CRN’s Most powerful women of the channel 2021: Power 100 list. To help amplify this great news and show our appreciation for all their hard work, Intel Business retweeted the announcement post and comments on CRN’s post with congratulatory copy. CRN engaged in return and increased visibility for this amazing award. Intel would not be successful without its successful employees so it’s important to honor them and put a face to the brand and all its successes.

 

  • Pat Gelsinger shared out a letter with the Intel Corporate Responsibility Report. One the first lines of the report focused on the importance of 5G. Rather than retweet the entire report and have viewers get lost in the report, Intel 5G Networks helped focus the POV to be more about 5G with a quote retweet of Pat’s letter. This quote can help remind viewers the importance of 5G and how it is one of Intel’s focus going forward without having to click in and read the entire report. Rather than coming from the brand, having this come from Pat Gelsinger directly has more weight and can help viewers understand and relate to the report more easily.

 

  • Intel and PING announced their partnership using Xeon Scalable processors to increase product performance and time to market. Intel HPC posted a quote retweet to highlight HPC solutions involved to better target this story to the correct audience. Intel HPC also engaged with PR posts to increase reach and showcase HPC solutions within the study. All interactions saw great engagement and is a great lead up to our upcoming original post that dives into this story in more details.

Glossary of Metrics

 

           

           

 

 

Posts: The number of posts published on Circuit+.

Viewrs: Unique users who saw at least one post on Circuit+.

Clicked: Percentage of viewrs who clicked at least one post.

Shares:  Number of share-action (employees sharing from Circuit+ to their personal social accounts).

Engagement:  Average of engagement (likes, comments, shares) inside Circuit+ across all posts.
